About Us

OCE Farms is a privately-owned farm. We specialize in organic, sustainably-grown produce, including fruits, vegetables, and animal farming. Our farm is located in the beautiful countryside of Itokin Ijebu, where we have access to clean air and water, and plenty of sunshine to grow our crops.

Our Products

At OCE Farms, we take pride in the quality of our produce. All of our fruits and vegetables are grown using natural methods, without the use of harmful pesticides or fertilizers. We offer a wide range of products, including:

  • Production of Fish
  • Consultancy
  • Processing of Fish
  • Farm Fresh Vegetables
  • Poultry Farming
  • Catfish Antibiotics
